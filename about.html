Our core services include water filtration, replenishment, and delivery. We take specific actions to make our water more potable. For example, we employ twin RO systems, water softeners, precision filters, activated carbon filters, quartz sand filters, and so on for filtration.

  • Quartz sand filter A booster pump is used to transfer turbid raw water from the raw water tank to the quartz sand filter. Organic debris, colloidal particles, and suspended solids are all efficiently retained and removed using a quartz sand filter.
  • Activated carbon filter Certain pollutants, particularly organic compounds are removed from water using activated carbon filters. As a result, the water becomes much more potable.
  • Softener The main purpose of softeners is to reduce the hardness of the water. The quantity of dissolved calcium, magnesium, and some other particles in the hard water is dropped as a result. This purifies the water of unwanted minerals.
  • Reverse osmosis It uses technology to purge most pollutants from the water. The germs are eliminated during the process, and the water is purified of any lingering microorganisms. It is more hygienic to drink reverse osmosis water. Double osmosis is far more advantageous than single osmosis. The penetrated water is transferred to a second RO unit for even more thorough purification.

These are some of the key procedures that we use at our firm to produce impurity-free water. As a result, we can give you the greatest product. We’re always here for you.
